Help & Support

Welcome to our Help Center. Here you’ll find answers to the most common questions, guidance on using our services, and ways to reach our support team for further assistance.


Frequently Asked Questions


How do I book a house?
To book a house, simply visit our Home Page, browse the available listings, and follow the on-screen instructions to complete your booking.


How do I reset my password?
You can easily reset your password by visiting the Account Settings page and selecting the "Reset Password" option. Follow the steps provided to create a new password securely.


How can I contact support?
Our support team is here to assist you. You can reach us anytime through our Contact Page or by emailing support@anex.com.


Where can I learn more about this site?
To learn more about our platform, mission, and team, please visit the About Us page.


Need More Help?
