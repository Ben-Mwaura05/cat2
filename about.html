About Anex Housing Agency


We believe everyone deserves a home they love. Whether you’re renting, buying, or just exploring options, our team is here to guide you with care, honesty, and expertise. Your comfort and satisfaction are at the heart of everything we do.

Get to know about Anex

Anex Housing Agency is a trusted real estate company dedicated to connecting clients with affordable, comfortable, and secure housing solutions. Since our establishment in 2015, we have proudly helped hundreds of families and individuals find their dream homes across Kenya. With a strong commitment to excellence and transparency, we continue to bridge the gap between property owners and tenants through innovative and reliable services.

Our mission and Our Vision

To be Kenya’s most trusted and preferred real estate partner — where every client finds a place they can proudly call home.

To simplify the housing process by providing transparent, reliable, and customer-friendly real estate services to tenants, landlords, and property investors across Kenya.

Our Services

  • Rental property listings and tenant placement
  • Property management and maintenance
  • Real estate sales and marketing
  • Tenant support and housing consultation
  • Investment advice for property owners
